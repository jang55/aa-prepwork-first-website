Why I love Fishing

Relaxing

It's an opportunity for me to get outdoor and see a different scenery instead of sitting down and looking at a computer screen or being locked in a room all day.

Family and friends

Going fishing with family and friends gives us an opportunity to spend time with one another and enjoy eachothers company. We can take time to catch up about life events, work life, home life, and etc.

Memories

The moments spending time with your loved ones will create memories for the rest of your life. I will never forget the day that my daughter caught her first fish at the age of 7. The way she smiled and jumped around in joy with full of happiness. Moments like that are to never be forgetton.
